Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026

Data Collection

KraftPad does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device.

What We Don't Do

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, usage data, or any other information. There are no third-party SDKs, trackers, or advertising frameworks in KraftPad.

Local Storage

KraftPad saves your preferences (theme, MIDI channel, key) locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your Mac.
